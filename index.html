01 / About

Who I Am

I'm Sahil, a software developer based in London, UK, currently in my final year of BSc Computer Science at Anglia Ruskin University. I like building things that actually work, from web platforms down to code that sits much closer to the hardware.

I tend to gravitate toward the parts of software that most people skip over: memory layouts, calling conventions, what the compiler decides to do with your code. It started as curiosity and turned into something I keep coming back to.

My background covers C# backend development, alongside low-level work in C++ and x86/x64 assembly picked up through personal projects. Most of what I know came from building things, watching them break, and figuring out why. A debugger and some patience will teach you more than documentation ever will.

Outside of a screen I travel often, and go down the occasional rabbit hole that has nothing to do with computers. I think that balance matters, as the best ideas usually come when you're not actively looking for them.
